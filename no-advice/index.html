“No Advice” is a small thing I’m doing to give back to the community.

I love meeting designers and entrepreneurs working on thoughtful solutions to compelling problems but reject the idea that giving advice is helpful outside of a meaningful relationship. So, while I can’t promise you “good advice,” I can promise a non-judgemental ear and some good stories!

If that sounds fun, let’s meet, and let me know if you’re in San Francisco - we may be able to meet in person. Choose a day below, and I’ll follow up via email. Talk soon!
